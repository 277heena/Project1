Swimming Lessons

Swim Lessons for Every Level

Splashes offers swimming lessons designed to help students become safer, stronger, and more confident in the water. Lessons are organized by age, ability, and comfort level.

Private Lessons

One-on-one instruction for students who need focused attention and faster progress.

Semi-Private Lessons

Small lesson format for students who benefit from both instruction and peer learning.

Parent & Me

Introductory classes for young children with parent participation and water comfort.
