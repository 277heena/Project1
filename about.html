About Splashes

Our Philosophy

Our mission is to educate and motivate individuals to be safer and learn proper swimming technique.

We believe there is a swimmer in every child. Splashes focuses on teaching the necessary skills needed to become a comfortable and confident swimmer right from the beginning.

Our instructors provide a positive environment where students can build safety, confidence, and technique while enjoying the learning process.

We Do A Pretty Good Job

Experience excellence through Splashes' swim programs where safety, progress, and a fun learning environment come first.
